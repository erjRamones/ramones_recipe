How to make Macaroni

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni, cooked according to package instructions
  • 1 cup cooked ham or chicken, diced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks, drained
  • 1 cup diced carrots, cooked until tender
  • 1 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked elbow macaroni, diced ham or chicken, pineapple chunks, cooked carrots, diced red bell pepper, diced cucumber, and sweet pickle relish.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, condensed milk, and white vinegar until well combined.
  3. Pour the dressing over the macaroni mixture in the large bowl.
  4. Gently toss all the ingredients together until evenly coated with the dressing.
  5. Season the macaroni salad with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the macaroni salad for at least an hour to allow the flavors to meld and the salad to chill.
  7. Once chilled, give the macaroni salad a final toss before serving.
  8. Transfer the macaroni salad to a serving dish and enjoy as a delicious side dish or light meal!
