How to make Pinakbet

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)
  • 1 medium-sized eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bitter melon (ampalaya), sliced
  • 1 squash, peeled and cubed
  • 10-12 pieces okra, ends trimmed
  • 1 cup water or vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, then add onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Add bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) and stir well to incorporate.
  5. Add eggplant, bitter melon, squash, and okra to the pan, stirring to coat the vegetables with the sauce.
  6. Pour water or vegetable broth into the pan and bring to a simmer.
  7. Cover and let the vegetables cook for about 10-15 minutes or until they are tender, stirring occasionally.
  8. Once the vegetables are cooked through, taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  9. Transfer to a serving dish and serve hot with steamed rice.
  10. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious Pinakbet!
