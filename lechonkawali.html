How to make Lechon Kawali

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, skin-on, cut into serving pieces
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for boiling
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork belly, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, and salt.
  2. Add enough water to cover the pork belly.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  4. Remove the pork from the pot and drain excess liquid. Let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Heat cooking oil in a deep frying pan or pot over medium heat.
  6. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the pork belly pieces skin side down into the hot oil.
  7. Fry until the skin becomes golden and crispy, and the meat is cooked through, about 8-10 minutes per side.
  8. Remove the crispy pork belly from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  9. Slice the Lechon Kawali into serving pieces and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
