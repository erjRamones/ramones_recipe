How to make Buko Pandan

Ingredients

  • 2 cups young coconut (buko), shredded
  • 1 package (24 grams) pandan-flavored gelatin
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 ounces) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream or all-purpose cream
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pandan-flavored gelatin according to package instructions. Allow it to cool and set, then cut it into small cubes.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded young coconut (buko) and pandan-flavored gelatin cubes.
  3. Add the sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and heavy cream (or all-purpose cream) to the bowl.
  4. Gently fold all the ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Transfer the Buko Pandan mixture into a serving dish or individual dessert cups.
  6. Optional: Garnish the top of the Buko Pandan with sweetened shredded coconut for added texture and flavor.
  7. Refrigerate the Buko Pandan for at least a few hours or until well chilled.
  8. Once chilled, serve the Buko Pandan cold and enjoy its refreshing and creamy goodness!
