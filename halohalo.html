How to make Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice
  • Sweetened red beans (cooked)
  • Sweetened garbanzos (cooked)
  • Kaong (sugar palm fruit)
  • Macapuno (coconut sport)
  • Nata de coco (coconut jelly)
  • Sago (tapioca pearls)
  • Sliced ripe jackfruit (optional)
  • Sliced sweetened plantains (optional)
  • Evaporated milk
  • Leche flan (caramel custard)
  • Ube (purple yam) ice cream (or any flavor of your choice)
  • Pinipig (toasted rice flakes) for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Fill a tall glass or bowl with shaved ice.
  2. Add layers of sweetened red beans, sweetened garbanzos, kaong, macapuno, nata de coco, sago, sliced ripe jackfruit, and sliced sweetened plantains (if using), distributing each ingredient evenly.
  3. Drizzle evaporated milk generously over the shaved ice and ingredients.
  4. Top the Halo-Halo with a slice of leche flan.
  5. Add a scoop of ube ice cream or your preferred flavor on top of the leche flan.
  6. If desired, sprinkle pinipig (toasted rice flakes) over the ice cream for added texture.
  7. Insert a spoon or straw into the glass or bowl and mix all the ingredients together thoroughly before enjoying!
