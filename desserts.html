Halo Halo

Halo-Halo is a beloved and iconic Filipino dessert that translates to "mix-mix" in Tagalog, which perfectly describes
its colorful and diverse ingredients. This refreshing dessert is a delightful combination of shaved ice layered with an
assortment of sweet beans, fruits, jellies, and other ingredients, topped with ice cream, leche flan (caramel custard),
and a drizzle of evaporated milk.

Leche Flan

Leche Flan is a classic and indulgent Filipino dessert that showcases the rich and creamy flavors of caramel custard.
Also known simply as "flan" or "creme caramel" in other parts of the world, Leche Flan has become a beloved staple in
Filipino cuisine, often served during special occasions, celebrations, and family gatherings.

Buko Pandan

Buko Pandan is a delightful and refreshing Filipino dessert that combines the flavors of young coconut (buko)
and pandan, a fragrant leaf commonly used in Southeast Asian cuisine. This dessert is loved for its vibrant green color,
tropical taste, and creamy texture. Some variations of Buko Pandan may include additional ingredients such as tapioca
pearls or nata de coco (coconut jelly) for added texture, or slices of ripe mango or pineapple for extra sweetness
and tanginess.

Puto

Puto is a traditional Filipino steamed rice cake that is enjoyed as a snack or dessert. These small, fluffy cakes are
popular throughout the Philippines and come in various flavors and colors. Puto is made from a simple batter consisting
of rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and water or coconut milk. The batter is mixed until smooth and then poured into
individual molds, often small round cups made from aluminum or silicone.

Macaroni

Macaroni is a type of pasta characterized by its short, curved shape with a hollow center. It is made from durum wheat
semolina flour and water, similar to other types of pasta like spaghetti and penne. Macaroni is a versatile ingredient
that is commonly used in a wide range of dishes, both savory and sweet. In sweet dishes, macaroni can be used in
desserts such as macaroni pudding or macaroni fruit salad, where it is combined with sweetened milk, fruits,
and sometimes nuts or marshmallows to create a creamy and indulgent treat.
