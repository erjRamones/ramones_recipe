How to make Bulalo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shanks, bone-in
  • 1 onion, peeled and quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2-3 pieces corn on the cob, cut into thirds
  • 1 small cabbage, quartered
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and quartered (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the beef shanks, onion, garlic, and enough water to cover the meat.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the beef is tender. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  3. Add the corn on the cob and continue to simmer for another 10-15 minutes, or until the corn is tender.
  4. If using potatoes, add them to the pot and simmer for an additional 10 minutes, or until the potatoes are cooked through.
  5. Add the cabbage quarters to the pot and cook for another 3-5 minutes, or until the cabbage is just tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot in bowls, making sure to include a piece of beef shank, corn, cabbage, and optional potatoes in each serving.
