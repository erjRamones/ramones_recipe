How to make Puto

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional, for topping)
  • Salted egg slices (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the rice flour, granulated sugar, and baking powder. Mix well to ensure even distribution of the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the water and evaporated milk to the dry ingredients. Mix until a smooth batter forms. The consistency should be similar to pancake batter.
  3. Prepare the steamer by bringing water to a boil.
  4. Meanwhile, grease the molds of a puto mold or muffin tin with oil or cooking spray.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased molds, filling each mold about three-quarters full.
  6. Optional: Top each filled mold with grated cheese and a slice of salted egg for added flavor and decoration.
  7. Once the water in the steamer is boiling, carefully place the filled molds into the steamer basket.
  8. Cover the steamer with a lid and steam the puto for about 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Once cooked, remove the puto from the steamer and let them cool slightly before unmolding.
  10. Serve the puto warm or at room temperature, and enjoy their soft and fluffy texture as a snack or dessert!
