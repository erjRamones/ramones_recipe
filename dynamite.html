How to make Lumpia Dynamite

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces large green chili peppers (siling pangsigang)
  • 100g cheese (cheddar or mozzarella), cut into strips
  • 10 pieces lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Wash the green chili peppers and pat them dry. Make a slit along one side of each chili pepper, removing the seeds and membranes if desired.
  2. Stuff each chili pepper with strips of cheese. Be careful not to overstuff to prevent the cheese from oozing out during frying.
  3. Wrap each stuffed chili pepper with a lumpia wrapper, ensuring that the cheese is fully enclosed.
  4. Secure the ends of the lumpia wrapper with a bit of water to seal them shut.
  5. Heat cooking oil in a deep frying pan or pot over medium heat.
  6. Carefully add the wrapped chili peppers to the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  7. Once cooked, remove the dynamite lumpia from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve the dynamite lumpia hot as a snack or appetizer, accompanied by a dipping sauce of your choice.
