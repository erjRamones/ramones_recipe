How to make Tinolang Manok

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 thumbs ginger, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cups spinach leaves
  • 2 pieces green papaya or chayote squash, peeled and sliced
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Pour water and chicken broth into the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Add green papaya or chayote squash slices and cook until tender, about 5-8 minutes.
  6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Add spinach leaves and cook for another 2-3 minutes or until wilted.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot in bowls.
  9. Enjoy your comforting and nutritious Tinolang Manok!
