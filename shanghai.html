How to make Lumpia

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork or beef
  • 1 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup finely chopped garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lumpia wrappers (spring roll wrappers)
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground pork or beef, finely chopped carrots, onions, and garlic.
  2. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Take a lumpia wrapper and place it on a flat surface.
  4. Spoon about 1-2 tablespoons of the filling onto the center of the wrapper.
  5. Fold the bottom edge of the wrapper over the filling.
  6. Fold the sides of the wrapper towards the center.
  7. Roll the wrapper tightly to enclose the filling, making sure there are no air pockets.
  8. Seal the edge of the wrapper with a little water to secure the lumpia.
  9. Heat cooking oil in a deep frying pan or pot over medium heat.
  10. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the lumpia in the pan, seam side down, in batches.
  11. Fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy, turning occasional.
  12. Once cooked, remove the lumpia from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  13. Serve the Lumpiang Shanghai hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as sweet chili sauce or vinegar dipping sauce.
